This project has two sections.
- The first section vreated in april 2021 looked at admissions as percentage of total infections.
- The second section looks at in hospital aspects of the COVID-19 pandemix, and tracks hospital beds, length of stay, % ICU, daily mortality over time. This section has resulted in a publication in the Dutch medical journal. See -> https://www.ntvg.nl/system/files/publications/D6/d6624.pdf
  The detailed methods can also be found online -> https://www.ntvg.nl/system/files/2022-01/D6624%20supplement%20def.pdf


The second section is currently active in terms of research and kept up-to-date, the first section is for legacy purpose only.
